Privacy Policy for Awesome Flashlight

1. Introduction

Awesome Flashlight is a flashlight application developed by Nir Moshe. This Privacy Policy describes how Awesome Flashlight collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the Awesome Flashlight app, such as the features you interact with and the settings you choose.
Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as your device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
Crash Data: In the event of a crash or error, we may collect information related to the crash, including device information and crash logs.
3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide and Improve the Service: We use the information collected to provide and maintain the Awesome Flashlight app, as well as to improve our services and develop new features.
Analytics: We may use third-party analytics tools to analyze usage patterns and trends in order to better understand how users interact with our app.
Customer Support: We may use the information to respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Awesome Flashlight app or provide services on our behalf.
Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.
5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

Awesome Flashlight is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated Privacy Policy, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.

8. Contact Us
